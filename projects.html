Neurologic Rehabilitation Institute of Ontario NRIO

Mississauga

NRIO is a private brain injury rehabilitation facility, specializing in the assessment, treatment and care of individuals who have sustained a traumatic brain injury, stroke or other neurological illness by increasing their level of independence.

ARCHITtheque was retained as the architect to assist the planners, Nick Dell Consulting, to design the renovation to NRIO Mississauga at 1320 Woodeden Drive, for compliance to Ontario Building Code. As part of the upgrades, the facility is made accessible, sprinklers added, fire rated separations added, to make the facility safe and accessible.​

  • Date: August 2018
  • Client: Neurologic Rehabilitation Institute of Ontario NRIO, Mississauga.
  • Category: Instututional

Health Clinics at Oakville and Burlington, Halton Region - Security and Access control

Halton Region

The Halton region Health department operates health clinics on South Service Road in Oakvilleat and on Fairview Street in Burlington. ARCHITtheque was retained by the Region to provide consultancy services for improvement to the security and access control throughout both locations. The goal was to attain Region's 'double-locking' requirements. Both the clinics are been upgraded to fully barrier free accessibility as per the latest code requirements.

  • Date: August 2018
  • Client: Health Clinics at Oakville and Burlington, Halton Region - Security and Access control
  • Category: Instututional

Queen's College of Business, Technology, and Public safety, Mississauga

Mississauga

The Queen's College opened their Mississauga location in 2016 at 121 Brunel Road. It offers Computer networking and Security, Police Foundations, etc. courses to Canadian and International students.

ARCHITtheque was retained as the architect by the Owner to provide their professional services in form of code review, permit submission and review during construction.

The college needed additional space for expanding and was offered by the Owner additional space at 111 Brunel Road. ARCHITtheque is associated in this expansion project in similar capacity.

  • Date: August 2018
  • Client: Queen's College of Business, Technology, and Public safety, Mississauga
  • Category: Instututional

Gymnasium - Auditorium Flooring Halton Region and Halton Police Services

Halton Region and Halton Police Services

The Halton Regional Centre Gymansium - Auditorium is a multi-purpose space that is used for sporting events, training classes, programs, meeting and conferences, social and community gatherings , farmers' market and various other events.

ARCHITtheque was retained to assist in selection of the new flooring that would not only meet the sports flooring standard, but also be able to withstand the use as farmers' market, meeting and conferences. As the space is booked in advance, the construction; removal of existing vinyl flooring, preparing the base and installation of new flooring was completed during the holidays (Dec 2106-Jan 2017) with ARCHItheque involved in Tendering and construction administration for the project.

  • Date: August 2018
  • Client: Halton Police Services
  • Category: Instututional

SAAAC - South Asian Autism Awareness Centre

Scarborough

SAAAC, created with the intention of inspiring families impacted by ASD with a special focus on the cultural elements in the South Asian community that pose particular challenges, has now embraced all communities and needed to expand, They moved to this newly designed 11,000 sft facility at 705 Markham Road, Scarborough, which has 8 one-on-one Training rooms, 4 ADE rooms, a Sensory therapy room, a Computer lab and a Gymnasium along with offices, board room for staff and a Social Entertainment room.

ARCHITtheque was retained as the architect to design, acquire permits and administer the contract. The project was completed in July 2018.​

  • Date: August 2018
  • Client: SAAAC - South Asian Autism Awareness Centre
  • Category: Instututional

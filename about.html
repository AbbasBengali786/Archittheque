About ARCHITtheque

ARCHITtheque is an architectural firm focused on work that is relevant, design that is functional, be it new construction, adaptive reuse or renovations. The firm is devoted to provide thoughtful, quality and responsible design that addresses the requirement and aspiration, and is responsive to the social context.

Our collaborative approach engages the client and consultants during the entire design process. We also believe that contractors, crafts persons, vendors and all other participants can contribute at all levels of the design process. A patient, considerate process with respectful exchanges between all involved leads to the best decision making and the desirable outcome.

The process is driven not by style, but by an early analysis and interpretation of the client’s needs through careful consideration of the site and program, exploration of space, materials and creative ways to meet the budget. We search to develop clear, distinguished work informed by the craft of construction, sustainability, culture and technology. It is our belief that every project is unique, thus each design response should be diverse and a reflection of the participants involved. It is our goal to exceed the client’s vision despite the most stringent budgets.

Services provided by ARCHITtheque are based on and appropriate to the project requirements.

The traditional architectural services, which address the design and construction of building, can be listed in its sequential phases as,

  • Schematic Design

  • Design Development

  • Contract Documents (drawings and specifications)

  • Bidding and Negotiation

  • Construction or Contract Administration

About the Founder

ARCHITtheque is founded by Hitesh Khatri, a registered architect with Ontario Association of Architects and has the Certificate of Practice in Ontario.

After completing his Bachelor Degree in Architecture in 1986 from Bengal Engineering College under Calcutta University, Hitesh has worked in India, Sultanate of Oman and Canada.

With strong design and technical skills background, Hitesh has, throughout his career, implemented every project with his pro-active and energetic approach, with due attention to detail. Regardless of the project size or type, he has come up with functional design, advancing sustainable practices in design and construction, and aesthetically pleasing built environment. He is capable of delivering your project to meet your need and budget, exceeding your expectations.

Hitesh has always been a champion for 'green, buiding strategies and is LEED Accredited Professional. All projects, whether by intention of registering for LEED certification or not, are designed, specified, constructed to acheive sustainibility.The built environment enhances the life of the people who inhabit and experience these, in profund and powerful ways.

With 10 years of extensive experience in Canada, he established ARCHITtheque to pursue his own practice and contribute to the society and the planet.
